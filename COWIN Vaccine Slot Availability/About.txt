A python script which will help you to get vaccine slots availability alerts for any location in India whenever free slots are available.
You have to provide the State name, District name, Age category and Dose number(1st or 2nd) as parameters.
If any slots are found it will check only after 20 seconds** if no slots were available at that time it will check again after 5 seconds**
and it will give a notification sound** when slots are available.

**all theses can be changed
